The Story Behind SSSuniverse

Hi, I'm Sahim Sonde. I believe that the best stories are found where the road ends and the trail begins. Growing up surrounded by the history of Raigad and the energy of Pune and Mumbai, I learned early on that beauty is earned through hard work.

Whether I’m scaling a fort in the Sahyadris or navigating a bustling market in Southeast Asia, my goal is simple: to travel smarter, trek harder, and help you find the hidden gems of this world.

My Philosophy

"Trekking isn't just about reaching a peak; it's about the discipline of the climb. I bring that same hardworking energy into everything I do—from planning complex international itineraries to testing gear in the pouring monsoon rains."

Local Expertise

Deep roots in Maharashtra give me a unique edge in scouting the best, least-crowded monsoon trails.

Global Vision

From the temples of Indonesia to the landscapes of Turkey, I curate adventures that balance budget with experience.

Honest Reviews

No fluff. Just field-tested gear advice that helps you prepare for the real world, not the showroom.
